About Moonlight Masq

Welcome to M88 Foxenigma's Moonlight Masq! In this game, your goal is to survive the Moonlight Masquerade for 60 seconds to win.

Fight the Foxenigma to survive and utilize special skills. Fill the ultimate bar to unleash a powerful attack that will eliminate all enemies on the screen.

Are you ready for the challenge? Put on your masquerade mask and face the Foxenigma under the moonlight!
