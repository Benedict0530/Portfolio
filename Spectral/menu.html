Game Information

Welcome to Soldier Spectral Retreat! This action-packed game challenges you to navigate through intense stages, defeat enemies, and conquer the boss fight using joystick controls.

Controls:

  • Use the joystick to move your character.
  • Press the joystick button to shoot.
  • Survive each stage to progress to the next level.
  • Conquer the boss fight to emerge victorious!

Good luck, soldier!
